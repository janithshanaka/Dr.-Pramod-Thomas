Our Services

Comprehensive dental solutions tailored to your unique needs — from routine care to complete smile transformations.

Braces / Orthodontics

Straighten your teeth and correct bite issues with our modern orthodontic solutions, including Fastbraces for faster results.

Read More

Our orthodontic services include traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and Fastbraces technology. Fastbraces use a patented bracket system that moves both the crown and root of teeth simultaneously, often achieving results in about half the time of traditional braces. We treat patients of all ages and offer flexible payment plans to make orthodontic treatment accessible for every family. During your free consultation, Dr. Thomas will evaluate your bite, discuss your goals, and recommend the best treatment path for you.

Dental Implants

Restore missing teeth with permanent, natural-looking dental implants that function and feel like your own teeth.

Read More

Dental implants are the gold standard for tooth replacement. Made of biocompatible titanium, they fuse with your jawbone to create a permanent, stable foundation for replacement teeth. Unlike dentures, implants don't slip or shift — they look, feel, and function like natural teeth. Dr. Thomas has extensive experience placing single implants, implant-supported bridges, and full-arch restorations. The procedure is comfortable, and most patients are amazed at how natural the results look and feel.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with veneers, teeth whitening, bonding, and other cosmetic procedures tailored to your goals.

Read More

Our cosmetic dentistry services are designed to help you achieve the smile you've always wanted. We offer professional teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, dental bonding, gum contouring, and complete smile makeovers. Each treatment plan is customized to your unique goals and facial aesthetics. Dr. Thomas uses the latest materials and techniques to deliver results that look natural and last for years. Whether you want to fix a single imperfection or completely transform your smile, we can help.

General Dentistry

Preventive care, cleanings, fillings, root canals, and more to keep your oral health in top condition for life.

Read More

General dentistry forms the foundation of good oral health. Our comprehensive services include routine exams and cleanings, digital X-rays, fillings, root canal therapy, extractions, periodontal (gum) treatment, and emergency dental care. We focus on prevention first — catching problems early saves you time, money, and discomfort. Our gentle, thorough approach ensures that patients of all ages feel comfortable and well-cared-for during every visit.

Invisalign

Achieve a straighter smile discreetly with Invisalign clear aligners — no metal brackets, no wires, just results.

Read More

Invisalign uses a series of custom-made, virtually invisible aligners to gradually shift your teeth into perfect alignment. The clear plastic trays are removable, so you can eat, drink, brush, and floss normally. Most adults and teens are great candidates for Invisalign. Treatment typically takes 6 to 18 months, and you'll see progress with each new set of aligners. Schedule a free consultation to find out if Invisalign is right for you.
